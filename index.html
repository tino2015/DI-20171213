ABOUT US

Delphine Interiors offers you a complete design service, creating personalised interiors for your residential or commercial projects.

Located in London with a French touch, Delphine has over 7 years’ experience working within interior design companies in Bordeaux, Paris and London.

With a real passion for space planning, textures, colours, and lighting; Delphine Interiors will design your home to be beautiful yet functional, whilst ensuring your individual needs are met.

Each project big or small will be a different adventure and a different result.

HOW WE WORK

Initial Consultation

Initial Consultation

The initial consultation is to learn about you, your project, and to feel comfortable with your future interior designer.

It's important for us to understand your personal tastes, lifestyle, expectations, and inspirations. This helps to create the right design for your daily life.

We will take a detailed brief, get a feel for the space, and establish a scope of work together. We'll also talk about your budget and others involved in the project, such as your architect, builder, electrictian, etc.

It’s also the perfect time for you to ask any questions about the design process and learn more about similar past projects and experience.

The fee for this consultation is a pre-agreed fee to only cover travel expenses if applicable.
Please note that London zones 1 to 4 are free of charge.

Concept

Following After receipt of my proposal with the scope of works that we have established in the first consultation and that you agreed and paid for, the next stage will be for DELPHINE INTERIORS to come on site to take full measurements, take some pictures that will be used for the next presentation. If the site is not accessible then, DELPHINE INTERIORS can work on architect’s plan or state agent’s before realising full survey once possible.  

The fee for this stage is £80.00 per hour including VAT.   

A presentation will be arranged in a 2nd meeting to present the concept design of the project including general plans, samples, drawings, sketches and a selection of various products.

Technical

Technical

Once approved and signed off, mood boards, technical drawings and detailed drawings will be realised (example for a kitchen project: the package will include the mood board with samples of colours, paint, wallpaper, kitchen plan and elevations, kitchen lighting plan, appliances specifications, products details i.e bar stools…).  

This package is then ready and can be given to your own contractor (carpenters, plumbers, decorators, electricians). includes all schedules that the contractors might required such as decorative schedule for the decorator, indicative lighting plan for the electrician  

DELPHINE INTERIORS will be able to recommend some contractors contacts if the client doesn’t have their own. However, the client will be charged by them directly.  

The fee for this package is £70.00 per hour including VAT.

Procurement

This 3rd meeting will include a full estimate of the products presented then selected on stage 2 together with the amended drawings for the client’s approval.

DELPHINE INTERIORS proposes a design and supply project only.

The designer will continue to monitor the progress of each i tem and schedule them for delivery to site when required  

All goods within the design scheme can be purchased directly through DELPHINE INTERIORS.
